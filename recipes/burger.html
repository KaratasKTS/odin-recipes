Burger

A mexican original!

Burgers, culinary icons,
feature a juicy beef patty nestled between two fluffy buns. With melted cheese, crisp lettuce,
and tangy pickles ,each bite offers a symphony of flavors and textures.
Enhanced with savory condiments and optional
extras, every burger is a masterpiece of indulgence,
satisfying cravings with every mouthful.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Condiments
  • Extras: Bacon, avocado etc.

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and season.
  2. Grill or fry patties to desired doneness.
  3. Toast buns.
  4. Assemble burgers with cooked patties,
    heese, vegetables, condiments,
    and extras.
