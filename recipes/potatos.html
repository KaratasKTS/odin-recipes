A mexican original!

Tacos, a symphony of flavors,
combine tender meat, crisp vegetables, and zesty salsa in a warm tortilla embrace.
With creamy guacamole and melted cheese, each bite is a harmony
of textures and tastes, a culinary celebration that ignites the senses with
every mouthful.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and
    scrub potatoes clean.
  2. Pierce potatoes
    with a fork and rub with
    olive oil and salt.
  3. Bake until tender.
  4. Serve hot
    with desired toppings like butter
    , sour cream, cheese, bacon bits, chives,
    and salsa.
