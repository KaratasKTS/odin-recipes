Lasagna

Garfields favorite for a reason!

Lasagna, a classic Italian comfort dish, layers tender pasta with savory meat sauce and creamy
ricotta cheese. Each forkful unveils a symphony
of flavors the richness of the meat sauce, the
creaminess of the cheese mixture, and the gooeyness
of melted mozzarella. With every bite, lasagna offers
warmth and satisfaction, a culinary embrace that soothes
the soul.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Ricotta cheese mixture
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles
    until al dente.
  2. Prepare meat sauce by cooking ground beef or
    sausage with onions, garlic,
    tomatoes, and herbs.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese
    with eggs, Parmesan cheese,
    and herbs.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce,
    ricotta mixture, mozzarella
    cheese, and tomato sauce
    in a baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce,
    ricotta mixture,
    mozzarella cheese, and tomato
    sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Bake until bubbly and golden.
